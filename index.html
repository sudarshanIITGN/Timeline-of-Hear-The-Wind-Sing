Timeline

Hear The Wind Sing/Pinball

Murakami's novels are a treasure trove of references to get lost into. This is a timeline cataloging every reference to songs, books and movies made by him in his novel Hear the Wind Sing/Pinball for a smooth nagivation of the story. Refernces are followed by what they are, the page number they appeared in and the corpus of text. I hope this interests atleast one person to read his novels! Books and Authors are in Light Brown. Singers, Composer and Albums are in Sea-green. Movies, Directors and TV shows are in Rose-Violet

I have no intention of using my writing to detail each painstaking step in this erosion. That would be a waste of my time. Those of you who want to read about that should turn to Romain Rolland’s Jean-Christophe. It is all written there."
